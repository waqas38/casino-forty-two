Casino de Monte-Carlo

The Epitome of Luxury in Monaco

Located in the heart of Monaco, Casino de Monte-Carlo has been a symbol of luxury and sophistication for over a century. Known for attracting the world's wealthiest individuals, it boasts opulent architecture and world-class gaming.

Location:Monaco
Built:1863
Features:Elegant architecture, High-stakes gaming, Live entertainment
Why Visit:Experience the glamorous lifestyle of Monaco's elite and indulge in luxury beyond imagination.

The Venetian Macao

Gondolas and Gambling in Macau

The Venetian Macao is the largest casino in the world, offering a stunning Venetian-inspired architecture. Guests can enjoy gondola rides, luxury shopping, and world-class gaming all under one roof.

Location:Macau, China
Built:2007
Features:Canals with gondola rides, Grand casino floor, Luxury shopping
Why Visit:Combine the excitement of gambling with the magic of Venice, right in the heart of Asia.
